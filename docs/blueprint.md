# **App Name**: कुलगुरु AI

## Core Features:

- Family Onboarding & Profile Management: Users can register, create and manage detailed profiles for themselves and their family members, including preferences like age and spiritual interests.
- AI-Powered Spiritual & Ethical Guidance: An interactive chat tool providing personalized AI guidance on dharma, ethics, life's challenges, and cultural practices, adaptable to specific religious or philosophical viewpoints chosen by the user.
- Interfaith Knowledge Center: A comprehensive, searchable repository offering information and comparative studies on various religions, scriptures, moral concepts, and scientific perspectives.
- Personalized Content & Daily Wisdom: The AI dynamically delivers tailored daily thoughts, spiritual affirmations, relevant short stories for children, and suggested family activities based on individual and family profiles.
- Community Support Forum: A platform for users to engage in discussions, post questions, share experiences, and receive peer advice within a moderated online community.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Saffron (#FF4F00), symbolizing energy and knowledge. It's vibrant yet grounded, reflecting the pursuit of wisdom.
- Background color: Ivory White (#F8F4E9), chosen for purity and simplicity, providing a clean canvas for content and evoking a sense of tranquility.
- Accent color: Golden Yellow (#FFB800), representing divinity and prosperity, ideal for highlighting interactive elements and calls to action.
- Complementary color: Calm Blue (#1A4D8C), offering a contrast that signifies trust and seriousness, balancing the warmer tones.
- Tertiary color: Sage Green (#4A7856), bringing a natural touch that symbolizes life and balance, used for subtle highlights or secondary informational elements.
- Headings: 'Premchand' or 'Krutidev' (Devanagari calligraphy-style) for a distinct, cultural aesthetic. Body text: 'Inter' (grotesque-style sans-serif) for clean readability. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Utilize culturally rich religious symbols (like Om, Crescent, Cross) and intuitive icons (like🆘 for crisis, 🙏 for prayer) to ensure universal understanding and cultural resonance. Icons should be clear and minimalist to maintain simplicity.
- A clean, modular layout structured with cards and well-defined sections, ensuring intuitive navigation and scannability. Designed to be highly responsive across all devices, maintaining a consistent user experience.
- Subtle, culturally evocative animations: loading screens feature rotating Dharma Chakra or Om symbols, transitions emulate gentle temple curtain movements, and hover effects manifest as auspicious Kumkum or Chandan visual cues, enhancing the spiritual experience without being distracting.
